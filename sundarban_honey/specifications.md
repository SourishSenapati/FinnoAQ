# Sundarban Honey (Raw & Unfiltered) Project Specifications

## 1. Executive Summary

Develop a "Raw & Unfiltered" Sundarban Honey product with <19% moisture and zero added sugar/chemicals.
**Innovation**: Use vacuum-assisted desiccation at low temperatures (<40°C) to prevent HMF formation while stabilizing fermentation risk.

## 2. Product Standards (Natural)

- **HMF (Hydroxymethylfurfural)**: < 10 mg/kg (Indicator of freshness/heat damage)
- **Sucrose**: < 5% (Adulteration check)
- **Pollen Count**: > 25,000/g (Authenticity marker)

### 3. Viscosity & Rheology

- **Moisture Content**: 17% - 20% (Standard Grade for long shelf-life)
- **Rheological Model**: Non-Newtonian (Pseudoplastic) behavior
- **Viscosity**: 3000 - 6000 cPs @ 25°C

## 3. Process Engineering (Zero-Heat)

### A. Moisture Reduction

- **Method**: High-Vacuum Dehydration (25-30 mmHg) @ 35-38°C.
- **Alternative**: Desiccant Bed (Silica Gel/Zeolite) air circulation.
- **Rate**: 0.5% moisture loss per hour to prevent volatile loss.

### B. Natural Preservation

- **Anti-Fermentation**:
  - Reduce Water Activity (aw) to < 0.60.
  - **Additive**: Clove Extract (Eugenol) @ 200ppm (Natural antimicrobial).
  - **Cinnamon Infusion**: Optional variant for stability.

## 4. Cost Structure

- **Raw Honey Cost**: ₹180-220/kg (Apiary Gate)
- **Processing Cost**: ₹15/kg (Vacuum Energy)
- **Packaging**: ₹25/kg (Glass Jar)
- **Total Cost**: ~₹260/kg
- **Target Selling Price**: ₹600/kg (Premium Raw)

## 5. Simulation Goals

1.  **HMF Kinetics**: Predict HMF rise per hour at 35°C vs 45°C vs 60°C.
2.  **Moisture Diffusion**: Time required to drop from 24% to 19% under vacuum.
3.  **Fermentation Probability**: Yeast growth risk vs. Moisture % curve.
