# FINNO PROJECTS: Additive Strategy & Fidelity Analysis

This document details the **Clean Label Additives** used in each project and quantifies how close the final product is to the "Original/Natural" benchmark using a **Fidelity Score (0-100%)**.

---

## 1. TOOR DAL ANALOGUE

| Additive Type     | Natural Ingredient                    | Function                             | Fidelity Score |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------ | :----------------------------------- | :------------- |
| **Binder**        | **Sodium Alginate** (Seaweed Extract) | Replacing natural starch structure   | **92%**        |
| **Setting Agent** | **Calcium Lactate** (Fermented Dairy) | Controlled gelation (Heat stability) |                |
| **Flavor**        | **MSG** (Fermented Cane/Corn)         | Umami booster to mask starch         | **88%**        |
| **Color**         | **Turmeric Powder**                   | Natural yellow hue                   | **99%**        |

- **Fidelity Analysis:**
  - **Texture (90%)**: High fidelity in bite/chew. Slight difference in "mushiness" if overcooked.
  - **Flavor (88%)**: Very close savory profile, but lacks the deep "earthy" note of premium unpolished Tur.
  - **Overall Match: 89%** (Primary deviation is lack of skin/husk texture).

- **Fidelity Analysis:**
  - **Aroma (98%)**: Vacuum process retains volatile mangrove floral notes lost in heated honey.

  - **Overall Match: 97%** (Indistinguishable from fresh hive honey, but stable).

## 6. MUSTARD OIL (50% Price Check)

| Additive Type       | Natural Ingredient                     | Function                      | Fidelity Score |
| :------------------ | :------------------------------------- | :---------------------------- | :------------- |
| **Base Oil**        | **Rice Bran Oil** (Physically Refined) | Neutral carrier (Cheap)       | **N/A**        |

- **Fidelity Analysis:**
  - **Smell (95%)**: Allyl Isothiocyanate from Mustard EO makes it smell authentic.
